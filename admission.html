How to Apply for APPLICATION FORM

1. Online Application Form:

You can fill an Online Application form through:

  • A. Info - ACIPL Classroom & DLP by making for TSAKETEX-2025 appeared students).
  • B. Our website by making for TSAKETEX-2025 appeared students).

2. OMR Application Form:

You can collect Application form from Our Admission Office / Information Centers on TSAKETEX 2025 appeared students).

(You need not to purchase an OMR Application Form if you have already filled an Online Application form)

FOR COURSES WHERE ADMISSION MODE IS DIRECT

  1. You can fill Online Application Form two days before Class Commencement Date.
  2. You can also send completely filled OMR Application Form with recent Passport size color photograph to our Corporate Office (janakpuri) or it can also be submitted in person at our Admission Office OR Information Center Office.
  3. For smooth completion of Admission Process, your duly filled Application Form must reach us before the commencement of course.
